---
title: Organize Your Projects
description: A quick guide to managing projects in Maycon Mota docs.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a folder">
    Click "New Folder" in your project sidebar.
    Name it for the topic or team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move docs">
    Drag and drop documents into folders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename folders">
    Click the folder name to edit.
    Press Enter to save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate">
    Click "Invite" to add team members to your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
